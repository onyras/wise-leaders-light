The Methodology

Three architectures of leadership

Inner Architecture

Mind, nervous system, patterns, identity. We measure your stress response with 350+ biomarkers and build the inner stability that everything else rests on.

Organizational Architecture

Structure, governance, culture, decision flows. We examine how your company is built and where it creates unnecessary friction for you and your team.

Pressure Architecture

How you and your company behave under stress. We build systems that help you respond rather than react when it matters most.
