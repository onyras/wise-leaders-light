CEO Peer Groups

The old way stops working

Cadence

Two retreats a year, for life

Immersive weeks. A WhatsApp circle between them. You return to the same people.

Monthly calls, random turnover

The same stories on repeat. People cycle out when the company sells.

What you train

Your nervous system, your presence, your blind spots

Silence, breath, biology, composure. Skills you apply on Monday.

Other people's tactical problems

Useful, but doesn't touch why the same problems keep showing up.

Practice

Measured and repeatable

Diagnostics before and after. Daily protocols. Practices you keep for life.

No practice, just conversation

No diagnostics. No embodied training. The same ceiling year after year.

Circle

Application-only, hand-matched

Curated by experience and temperament. First 15 per retreat. Built to last.

Grouped by zip code

Anyone who pays. Random match. Surface stays surface.

What you leave with

A practice, a protocol, a circle

Skills in your body. A plan that fits your biology. CEOs who know you at your edges.

Notes and a contact list

Useful advice. But the person going back to the office is the same one who walked in.
