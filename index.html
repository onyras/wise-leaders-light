Be honest about what you need

You are a fit if

You lead a real company

  • Your company scaled past twenty people and you feel the leadership game changed faster than you did Yes
  • You are raising a new round, preparing for exit, or navigating a major transition and realize you need to evolve alongside it Yes
  • Your executive team is not executing at the level you need, or you sense your own leadership might be the constraint Yes
  • You have tried other approaches (coaching, courses, books, peer groups) but want something more rigorous, connected to real peers, and grounded in evidence Yes
  • You can commit to one full year
  • You're ready to do the hard work
  • You're open to honest feedback

You are not a fit if

You're looking for answers

  • You want a quick inspirational seminar instead of ongoing practice No
  • You prefer to outsource leadership issues to HR or consultants No
  • You are not willing to bring real situations into the room and be challenged by peers No
  • You are skeptical of measuring your own development or uncomfortable with data about your stress, recovery, and behavioral patterns No
  • You can't show up fully
  • You want to avoid hard work
  • You're not ready to listen
  • You're seeking quick fixes
